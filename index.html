Case Studies

How I work, project by project

A look behind the deliverables — the problem, the decisions I made, and what came out of it.

Brand & Social

BK-Nação — Healthcare brand & social

Marketing agency · dental & healthcare clients · Instagram, ads, brand
Challenge

Healthcare clients needed to build trust and turn scattered social feeds into real bookings — with a consistent, professional look across dozens of posts a month.

Approach

I built an on-brand visual system and a content calendar, then produced hook-first reels and ads in 1:1 and 9:16. I designed reusable templates so output stayed fast and consistent, and read the metrics to repeat what worked.

Outcome

A steady, recognizable content pipeline the clients could rely on — on-brand every time, quicker to produce, and tuned to engagement instead of guesswork.

3D · Architecture

Karpiel Steindel — Architecture visualization

International architecture studio · 3D visualization & client presentations
Challenge

Selling unbuilt buildings is hard — clients struggle to read plans and picture the finished space, which slows down approvals.

Approach

I produced photorealistic exterior and interior visualizations with deliberate lighting and composition, and built presentation boards that told the spatial story clearly. I iterated tightly with the architects on each design direction.

Outcome

Clients could finally see the vision, which made conversations faster and approvals more confident — presentation-ready assets for an international practice.

Video · Growth

True-story YouTube channels

Cidadãos Exemplares & Witness This Ca$e · editing, motion & thumbnails
Challenge

On faceless educational and true-story channels, the thumbnail and the first seconds decide everything — retention is the whole game.

Approach

I worked from research-driven scripts into evidence-paced edits with motion graphics and sound design, and designed bold custom thumbnails. I kept testing hooks and thumbnails to learn what held attention.

Outcome

100K+ views generated and steady community growth across channels, with a repeatable format the channels could keep scaling.

Web & UI

Landing pages & design systems

Campaign & brand websites · Figma, WordPress, HTML/CSS · conversion-focused
Challenge

Campaigns and brands needed landing pages that actually convert — and a way to keep look and message consistent across many pages without rebuilding from scratch every time.

Approach

I designed in Figma and built reusable components and a small design system, so pages share one consistent visual language. Responsive layouts in WordPress and HTML/CSS, structured around the hook, the offer and a clear call to action — with AI to spin up and test creative variations fast.

Outcome

On-brand pages that ship faster and read clearly on every screen — built for the metric, not just the look, and easy to extend as campaigns grow.

Content & Community

Mais Futebol-News — building a 20K+ community

Facebook football page · copywriting, daily content & community growth
Challenge

Grow a football page from scratch into a real, engaged community — and keep it active every single day in a crowded, fast-moving sports feed.

Approach

I researched, wrote and published daily posts built around timing, strong headlines and the conversations fans actually care about. I kept a consistent voice and posting rhythm, and read what landed to shape what came next.

Outcome

A sustained community of 20,000+ followers — proof I can build an audience, hold its attention over time and keep engagement alive, not just post once and hope.
